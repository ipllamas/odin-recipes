Alfredo Pasta


Description

Alfredo sauce is a favorite for many pasta-lovers. This rich and easy-to-make sauce will have you making it for dinner over and over again!

While tradtional Alfredo sauce only calls for parmesan and butter, we will be using cream, garlic, and other seasonings to add to the richness.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 2 Tsps Minced Garlic
  • 1/2 Tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1/4 Tsp Pepper
  • 2 Cups Freshly Grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Add the butter and cream to a large skillet.
  2. Simmer over low heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Whisk in the garlic, italian seasoning, and pepper for one minute.
  4. Whisk in the parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Serve immediately.
